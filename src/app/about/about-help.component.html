Saving Review as PDF

Directions

  1. After clicking the "Print!" button, a print page will appear.
  2. From the print page, Click on "Change..." under Destination in the top left side of the screen. A window will popup.
  3. Under "Local Destinations" click "Save as PDF." The window will close.
  4. In the top left side of the screen, click the "Save" button.


Web-App on Mobile

Android: Add to Homescreen

  1. Press top right button in navigation bar. The button is three stacked dots.
  2. Press "Add to Home Screen."

iPhone: Add to Homescreen

  1. Press middle button in navigation bar at bottom of iPhone screen. The button is an arrow pointing out of a box.
  2. Press "Add to Home Screen."
