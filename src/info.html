StudyMC

Info


StudyMC (Study Medicinal Chemistry) is a web application to help students in learning, testing, and reviewing their course materials.


  Learn

Content provided to help expand students' knowledge along with practice problems with feedback.

  Test

Example test questions to gauge a student's knowledge on a particular topic.

  Review

Printable online review sheet. The "Autocomplete" button on the right provides an example sheet.


How to Save Review Worksheet as a PDF Video

After clicking the "Print!" button, a print page will appear. From the print page, Click on "Change..." under Destination in the top left side of the screen. A window will popup. Under "Local Destinations" click "Save as PDF." The window will close. In the top left side of the screen, click the "Save" button.
